The Church of the Broccoli Fly

The Broccoli Fly?

Yes, The Broccoli Fly has created our universe (and all others, if the multiverse theory is correct). It watches over us, and is responsible for bringing life to earth.

The Broccoli Fly is God, according to the definition of god: 

(in Christianity and other monotheistic religions) the creator and ruler of the universe and source of all moral authority; the supreme being.

What is the Church of the Broccoli Fly?

The Church of the Broccoli Fly is a monotheistic, liberal religion. It does not impose restrictions, but it gives advice.

Of most importance is equality. Everyone is equal. Only the Broccoly Fly is better.

How can I join the religion?

You do not have to officially join the religion (the Broccoli Fly loves you anyway), but you can follow the @BroccoliFly on Twitter or "like" the Broccoli Fly's Facebook Page to be recognized as a worshipper.
